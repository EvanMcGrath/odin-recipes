Evan's Saucy Little Pizza Pies

My Saucy Little Margherita Pie

To make this Pizza you need to make the dough. That involves making a poolish. Mix half of your total flour with equal parts water and a scant 1/8th teaspoon and mix. Once well incorporated leave to ferment for 12-15 hours.

Once the 12-15 hours has passed and your poolish is active add the rest of your ingredients for the dough. Let the dough rise for another 4 hours and divide and separate into equal sized dough balls. Let proof for an hour. Then you are ready to bake.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Make the poolish
  2. Make the dough
  3. Let dough rise
  4. Shape into dough balls
  5. Proof dough balls
  6. Bake
